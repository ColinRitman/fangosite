    Our Vision

    Time is money. We believe that every form of time spent on fandom should be valued
    and therefore monetizable in fango by way of how much value it brings to another...
    From the sheer joy you experience when seeing an original piece of fan art or
    unique meme of your favorite comic book character or sports team, to the deeper levels
    of appreciation- such as for the person who can confirm & recommend that the time
    spent for you to watch Game Of Thrones seasons 1-7 would be worth it.

    For far too long, corporations & media conglomerates have undervalued and
    even ignored the immense amount of care, originality, & time spent on fandom.
    Now by utilizing the public & decentralized Fango privacy currency along with
    tools & platforms created by The Fandom Gold Project- we, the fans- as individuals,
    at a peer-to-peer level, are able to put the power of fandom back where it belongs for good

    - in the hands of each and every fan.
